Features

You can sell, invest, buy pokemons. Pokemons get valued based on how many battles they won, the higher the value of the pokemon they fight, the higher the reward when winning.

You have a Brokerdex with the pokemons you invested in

Each pokemon has their unique history in the simulated world

  • Pokemons may birth new pokemons into the market.
  • If the pokemon is on a winning streak since your investment, you earn a bonus each time they win more!
